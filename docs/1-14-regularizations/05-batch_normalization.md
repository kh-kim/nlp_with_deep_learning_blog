## 배치정규화의 동작

$$\begin{gathered}
\text{batch}\_\text{norm}(x)=\gamma\frac{(x-\mu)}{\sqrt{\sigma^2+\epsilon}}+\beta \\
\\
\mu=x.\text{mean}(\text{dim}=0) \\
\sigma=x.\text{std}(\text{dim}=0) \\
\\
\text{where }x\in\mathbb{R}^{N\times{n}}.
\end{gathered}$$
