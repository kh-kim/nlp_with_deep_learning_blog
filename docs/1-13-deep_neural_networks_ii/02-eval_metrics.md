# Evaluation Metrics

## Precision & Recall

$$\begin{gathered}
\text{Accuracy}=\frac{TP+TN}{TP+FP+FN+TN} \\
\text{Precision}=\frac{TP}{TP+FP} \\
\text{Recall}=\frac{TP}{TP+FN} \\
\end{gathered}$$

## F1-Score

$$\begin{gathered}
\text{F1-Score}=2\times\frac{\text{Recall}\times\text{Precision}}{\text{Recall}+\text{Precision}}
\end{gathered}$$
